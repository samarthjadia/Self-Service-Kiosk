Privacy Policy

Welcome to Coffee Maker. Coffee Maker was built by 6 NC State Computer Science students at no cost as part of a course project and is intended for use as is.

This page informs users about our policies regarding collecting, using, and disclosing Personal Information.

Choosing to use our service means agreeing to the collection and use of information in relation to this policy. The information collected is used to provide and improve our Coffee Maker service. Your information will not be shared with anyone except as our Privacy Policy describes.

Information Collection

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you register on our website, we ask for your username and password. When placing an order, we ask for your name.
  • Order Information: Details about the coffee that you purchase.

Use of Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To manage your account and provide support
  • To process and fulfill orders
  • To communicate with you about your orders or transactions
  • To improve and optimize our website and services provided

Information Sharing and Disclosure

Your information will not be shared with any third-party service providers.

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or deletion. However, no internet-based site can be 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

User Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information stored with us. You can manage your information through your account settings or by contacting us directly.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
